## Image Specifications
- **Format:** JPG
- **Dimensions:** 1200x630px (optimal for social sharing and blog headers)
- **Style:** Modern flat design, professional business aesthetic
- **Color scheme:** Primarily blue tones (matching brand), with accent colors as specified
- **Quality:** High resolution, web-optimized
- **Usage:** Blog post headers, social media sharing, featured images

## Notes
- All images should maintain consistent style across the blog
- Avoid text in images (text will be in HTML)
- Focus on visual metaphors and clear iconography
- Ensure images are culturally appropriate for Slovak/EU audience
- Images should work well in both light and dark modes
